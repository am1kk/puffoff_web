B. PERSONAL DATA PROCESSED

When you use the App, personal data may be processed. As the data controller, I process the following categories of personal data:

  • Identifiers – such as uid, device's advertising identifier, IP address.
  • Contact info – such as e-mail address only if you contact customer support.
  • Purchases information – such as payments related solely to the App.
  • User content – such as customer support requests.
  • Usage data – such as app launches, taps, scrolling information, or other information about how you interact with the App.
  • Diagnostics data – such as crash logs, timezone ID, language, iOS/Android version or other technical information about your device.
  • Customer support data – such as identification of issue, messages between you and me, attached files and additional information, information about resolution of your issues.
  • Marketing data – such as marketing ID, device information, information about viewed ads (only if ads are displayed in the App).

Please, do not use the App to upload any sensitive information about you, such as health data, genetic data, biometric data, data revealing racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, or data concerning sexual orientation.

The App is not designated for children. By downloading or installing the App, you declare that you are older than 13 years old and are solely responsible for any damage or loss that may result from an untruthful declaration.

Data processed: Identifiers, Purchases information, User content, Usage data, Diagnostics data, Customer support data.

Data processed: Identifiers, Purchases information, User content, Usage data, Diagnostics data, Customer support data.

E. YOUR RIGHTS IN PROCESSING AND HOW TO EXERCISE THEM

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent
  • Request rectification of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Request portability of your data
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority

You can exercise your rights by contacting me at support@puffpunk.app.

Requests will be processed without undue delay, within 1 month. In exceptional cases, this period may be extended by up to 2 months, and you will be informed accordingly.

F. RIGHT TO FILE A COMPLAINT

You also have the right to file a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority for data protection in your country of residence. For Portugal, this is the Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados (CNPD), located at Av. D. Carlos I, 134, 1200-651 Lisbon, Portugal.
